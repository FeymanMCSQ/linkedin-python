Let me tell you about “𝘔𝘦𝘥𝘈𝘐”—a fictional AI system designed to prioritize emergency healthcare during a devastating earthquake. As AI reshapes healthcare, finance, and governance, we’re not just coding systems—we’re encoding 𝘃𝗮𝗹𝘂𝗲𝘀. Here’s what we can’t ignore:  

🔍 𝗧𝗿𝗮𝗻𝘀𝗽𝗮𝗿𝗲𝗻𝗰𝘆  
𝘞𝘩𝘰’𝘴 𝘢𝘤𝘤𝘰𝘶𝘯𝘵𝘢𝘣𝘭𝘦 𝘸𝘩𝘦𝘯 𝘈𝘐 𝘮𝘢𝘬𝘦𝘴 𝘢 𝘣𝘪𝘢𝘴𝘦𝘥 𝘥𝘦𝘤𝘪𝘴𝘪𝘰𝘯? Black-box systems erode trust. Explainability must be woven into design.  

⚖️ 𝗘𝗾𝘂𝗶𝘁𝘆
